How it works

Built like a private briefing, not a blog.

The MargaZine is where Marga Partners thinks out loud about the industry, in depth, for the people building it. It is closed by design, and organized to be used.

01

Free, every Monday

A new piece publishes every Monday and lands in your inbox. One email is the whole price of admission; the essays stay free, and you can unsubscribe anytime.

02

One idea at a time

Every essay takes a single question apart slowly and completely, the way the industry rarely does. You leave understanding one thing you did not before.

03

A connected library

Every framework links to the outside cases that prove it, and every case links back to its framework. The whole publication is a network, not a feed.

04

Frameworks you can use

The proprietary lenses live inside the essays: the Trojan Horse Product, the Wrong Endpoint, Trust Compounds, the Market Trust Index, the Permission Gap. The tools behind them, the Marga Method and the Marga Index, are where the thinking becomes practice.
