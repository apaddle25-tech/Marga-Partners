Selected work

What aesthetics can learn from everyone else.

Every hard problem in this industry has already been solved somewhere else. A sample of the parallels we study and the strategies we bring back. Each one is the seed of a report we build for a client; the thinking behind them runs free in the MargaZine.

Example analysis

Make the molecule the reason to buy.

The parallel · Intel Inside

Intel took an invisible component and made buyers demand it by name, turning a spec sheet into pricing power. Aesthetics sells the finished brand while the molecule that actually differentiates stays anonymous. The move is to brand the active before a competitor claims it.

Turn a safety scare into the cheapest trust you will ever buy.

The parallel · Tylenol, 1982

Johnson and Johnson pulled every bottle after a tampering crisis, absorbed the loss, and set the standard for crisis response for forty years. Aesthetics manages every adverse-event story quietly, as PR. The parallel shows why over-responding to a trust shock compounds in your favor.

🔒 Full breakdown · Commission → Example analysis

Pull the hesitant majority into the category.

The parallel · Invisalign

Invisalign did not beat braces on price; it reframed orthodontics as an adult choice and removed the social cost of starting. Aesthetics fights over the penetrated few while the curious many sit out. Lower the perceived cost of the first step, not the price of the treatment.

🔒 Full breakdown · Commission → Example analysis

Hold a premium as the category commoditizes.

The parallel · Dyson

Dyson charged five times the going rate for a vacuum by making its engineering visible and its design desirable. Energy devices are sliding toward price parity as claims converge. Make the technology legible and the experience aspirational before the number is all that is left.
