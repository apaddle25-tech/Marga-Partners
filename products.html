The Marga Method

A defensible number, not a hot take.

Five forces decide whether an asset wins: Product, Patient, Provider, Price, and Practice. Each is scored 0 to 100 against anchored evidence, then weighted into a single composite, the Marga Index. The result shows exactly where an asset is strong and where it is exposed.
